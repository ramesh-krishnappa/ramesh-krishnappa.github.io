Emotional intelligence

What Is Emotional-Intelligence?

  • Emotional Intelligence (EI) is the ability to recognise both your own emotions and understand the emotions of people around you and to use that knowledge to guide our thoughts and behaviors.

    • What is the Difference Between IQ and EQ?

  • Intelligence quotient (IQ) is a score derived from standardized tests designed to measure intelligence.Your IQ relates directly to your intellectual abilities, like how well you learn as well as understand and apply information
  • Emotional intelligence (EQ) is like using emotions to think and enhance our reasoning. Those with high emotional intelligence are able to manage their emotions as well as use their emotions to facilitate their thinking and understand the emotions of others

    • Why Is Emotional Intelligence Important?

  • EQ is important because it helps you to build relationships, reduce team stress, defuse conflict and improve job satisfaction. And its also important for personal growth.
